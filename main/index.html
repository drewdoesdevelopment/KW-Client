Who We Are

About Us

Kenneth L. Wake, Jr. (Bud) was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas. He attended Judson High School and graduated from the University of Texas in San Antonio before entering Law School. He earned his Doctor of Jurisprudence degree from Saint Mary’s Law School and graduated with honors (Cum Laude). While pursuing his J.D., he worked for some local attorneys and took a job with an insurance defense firm for the first year after graduation. He then opened his own practice and has been helping people with their legal issues and developing long term relationships ever since.

Father's Rights

When parents are working through the divorce process, one of their primary concerns is protecting their relationship with their children. Many fathers fear that their children’s mothers will automatically receive custody, regardless of whether that is in the best interests of the children. It’s critical to work with an experienced lawyer who can help ensure that your rights and your relationship with your children are protected at every step of the process.
